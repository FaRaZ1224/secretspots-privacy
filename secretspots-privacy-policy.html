Privacy Policy for Secret Spots

This Privacy Policy describes how Secret Spots ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Secret Spots is a community-driven platform that helps users discover and share hidden, lesser-known locations around them.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: To use Secret Spots, you must create an account. We collect your email address and securely store your authentication information. You may also sign in using third-party social login providers (handled securely via Supabase Auth).
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide profile information such as your name, profile picture, and preferences.
  • User Content: We collect content you create, upload, or share on the App, including:
    • Photos of locations you submit
    • Descriptions of the locations
    • GPS coordinates (automatically extracted or manually added)
    • Category selections (Nature, Food & Drink, Art & Culture, Adventure & Activities)
    • Comments, ratings, and other interactions with spots
  • EXIF Metadata: Photos you upload may contain EXIF metadata, including GPS coordinates, date/time information, and device details. We use this data to help place locations on the map.
  • Communications: We may collect information when you contact us for support, report content, or participate in community discussions.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Location Information: With your permission, we collect your precise location when you use the App to show nearby spots, help you navigate to them, or autofill coordinates when uploading new spots. Location data is only collected when the app is in use (foreground), not in the background.
  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, including device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the App, including interactions with spots, searches, favorites, and features you use.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Create and maintain your account
  • Process and fulfill your requests
  • Show you spots near your location
  • Allow you to share and discover spots
  • Moderate content through NSFW image detection and user flagging
  • Ensure community guidelines are followed
  • Communicate with you about the App
  • Protect against harmful or unlawful activity

Content Moderation

To maintain a safe and appropriate environment, Secret Spots employs the following moderation measures:

  • NSFW Image Detection: All submitted images are analyzed using open-source NSFW detection tools before being published to the platform.
  • User Reporting: Users can flag inappropriate content, which triggers manual review.
  • Content Removal: We reserve the right to remove content that violates our community guidelines.

How We Share Your Information

We may share information as follows:

  • With Other Users: Content you post (including spots, photos, GPS coordinates, descriptions, and ratings) will be visible to other users of the App. This is the core functionality of Secret Spotsâ€”helping users discover locations shared by others.
  • Service Providers: We share information with third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf, such as:
    • Supabase (for secure data storage and authentication)
    • Image moderation systems (for NSFW content detection)
    • Hosting and cloud storage providers
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or if we believe it's necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.

Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Your data is stored using Supabase with encryption at rest
  • Authentication uses secure, modern practices
  • We regularly review our systems to identify vulnerabilities
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers

Your Choices and Rights

  • Account Information: You can update your account information through the App settings.
  • Location Information: You can control location permissions through your device settings. Note that some features may not function without location access.
  • Content Deletion: You can delete spots you've created, though this may not remove them from all backups or caches immediately.
  • Account Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data through the App settings or by contacting us.
  • Data Access: In some jurisdictions, you may have the right to request access to and receive a copy of your personal data.
  • Do Not Track: Our App does not respond to Do Not Track signals.

Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will delete or anonymize your information upon request unless we need to keep it for legitimate business or legal purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and processed in, countries other than your own. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from those in your country. We take steps to ensure adequate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
